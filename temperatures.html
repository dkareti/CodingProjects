Temperature Conversion

If you know the temperature in degrees Fahrenheit, then the conversion to degrees Celsius is fairly simple. All you have to do is follow a simple algebraic function. Let's assume that the temperature in fahrenheit is denoted by the letter x. There are only two steps to follow:
1. Subtract x by 32.
2. Then multiply the result by the fraction 5/9.
Following these steps will result in a value that is the celsius equivalent to the temperature in Fahrenheit.
